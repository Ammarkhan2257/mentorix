About Us

We create structured online teaching careers for Pakistani tutors.

Mentorix is built for hiring, training, and managing tutors locally in Pakistan. Our purpose is to give serious candidates a professional environment where they can teach online, improve their skills, and grow through a proper system.

We believe good tutors need more than subject knowledge. They need communication, patience, time management, lesson planning, and a professional attitude.
