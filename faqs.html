Common questions

Important answers for candidates before applying.

Is Mentorix based in Pakistan?

Yes, Mentorix is positioned as a Pakistan-based tutor hiring and training company.

Do I need experience?

Experience is preferred, but strong subject knowledge, English communication, and seriousness also matter.

Is training provided?

Selected candidates may receive training based on their role, experience, and performance.

Is night shift required?

Many roles may require evening or night availability because online teaching can follow international time zones.

Will there be a contract?

Selected candidates may complete joining documents and contract formalities.

How will HR contact me?

Shortlisted applicants may be contacted through WhatsApp, phone call, or email.
