FAQs

Common questions

Here are answers to common questions candidates may ask before applying.

Is this office-based?

Yes. Current roles are office-based in Lahore.

Is night shift required?

Yes, the main shift is 9:00 PM to 6:00 AM. A secondary shift from 8:00 PM to 2:00 AM may be available depending on need.

Can fresh candidates apply?

Yes. Fresh candidates can apply if they have good English communication and strong subject understanding.

What qualification is required?

Bachelor’s students and graduates can apply. Final suitability depends on subject knowledge, communication, and availability.

Do I need a laptop?

For office-based roles, equipment may be provided according to company setup. Basic computer/laptop understanding is still expected.

Is food or accommodation provided?

No, food and accommodation are not provided at the moment.

How will salary be decided?

Salary or compensation will depend on role, subject, experience, performance, and company requirements.

How will HR contact me?

Shortlisted applicants may be contacted through WhatsApp, phone call, or email.

Where is the office?

The office is located in Jia Musa, Lahore. Full address is available on the contact page.
