Contract Policy

Clear expectations before joining.

Mentorix may require selected candidates to complete joining documents, training confirmation, role expectations, and contract formalities before final appointment.

The purpose of the policy is to keep communication clear between the company and candidates before they start office-based teaching work.

Common policy areas

  • Role and responsibilities
  • Office-based shift timing
  • Attendance and punctuality
  • Training period and performance review
  • Confidentiality and student data protection
  • Notice period and exit process
  • Professional behavior and code of conduct
This website page gives a general overview only. Final contract terms, salary, joining date, training duration, and policies will be shared with selected candidates by management/HR.
