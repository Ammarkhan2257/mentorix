Hiring Process

How we select tutors

Our hiring process is designed to understand the candidate’s subject knowledge, communication, availability, and training needs.

01

Application Review

We review your basic information, qualification, city, subject, experience, and shift availability.

02

HR Screening

Shortlisted applicants may receive a call or WhatsApp message for initial screening.

03

Subject Interview

We discuss your subject confidence, teaching style, communication, and understanding of student needs.

04

Teaching Demo

Candidates may be asked to explain a topic in a short demo so we can review clarity and delivery.

05

Training

Selected candidates receive training support depending on their role, experience, and teaching readiness.

06

Final Selection

Final selection depends on communication, subject knowledge, demo, availability, and company requirements.
