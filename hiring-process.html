Process

Simple, serious, and professional

Our hiring process helps us select committed candidates who can follow a professional teaching model.

1

Application

2

Screening

3

Interview & Demo

Shortlisted candidates attend an interview and may present a teaching demo.

4

Training & Contract

Selected candidates complete training and proceed with joining formalities.
